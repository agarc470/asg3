Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Use the following controls:

  • W, A, S, D: Move camera forward, left, backward, right.
  • Q, E: Pan camera left and right.
  • B: Place a block at the current location.
  • R: Remove a block from the current location.
  • Shift + Mouse Click: Spin the world and change the skybox texture.

    • Welcome to the Mysterious World of Arcticania!
    You find yourself within an expansive skybox, standing on a sturdy floor bordered by dirt blocks under an ethereal sky. Before you stands a majestic wolf, guardian of this realm, its eyes gleaming with untold secrets.
    Before embarking on your quest, you have the power to transform your environment: a shift-click on the canvas will change the skybox to a dramatic lava texture, setting the stage for your adventure.
    Your mission is to explore this mysterious world and collect 10 elusive blocks scattered randomly across the land. Each block is cloaked in a bone texture, resonating with the theme of the world. Use R to remove them as you navigate this strange terrain.
    As you gather these blocks, the world around you will begin to transform. Upon collecting all ten, the skybox itself will change to reveal a stunning bone texture, signifying your mastery over this arcane environment.
    Ready your wits and prepare for an adventure like no other!
